Surface Duo 2

Microsoft Surface Duo 2 is the second generation of Microsoft's dual-screen Android device. It offers some key improvements over the original Surface Duo, incorporating customer feedback and modern technological advancements.

It features two separate screens that are connected by a 360-degree hinge, allowing you to use it in various modes such as laptop mode, tent mode, or folded back mode.
